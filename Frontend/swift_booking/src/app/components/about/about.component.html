About Swift Booking

Swift Booking is an appointment management service focused on small teams who need quick scheduling, clear customer history, and low admin overhead.

Feature Set

  • Live booking list with status filtering and search.
  • Dedicated booking creation and editing workflows.
  • Team-assigned appointments with employee visibility.
  • Simple account management and payment flow.

Subscription Model

The platform is available as monthly subscriptions with increasing limits and support levels. Start on Core, then move to Growth or Pro as your booking volume scales.
